Lyon Confluence

Lyon Confluence is a modern urban development located at the confluence of the Rhône and Saône rivers in Lyon, France. Known for its stunning architecture and innovative design, it has become one of the most popular shopping, dining, and cultural destinations in the city. With a mix of contemporary retail spaces, restaurants, and attractions, Lyon Confluence offers a vibrant atmosphere that perfectly blends modernity with Lyon’s rich history.

The Lyon Confluence district features a variety of shopping experiences, from luxury boutiques to trendy concept stores. Its waterfront location also adds to the area’s charm, providing beautiful views of the rivers and surrounding areas.

The shopping center at Lyon Confluence is home to a wide selection of brands, offering everything from high-end fashion to everyday essentials. It’s also known for its architectural brilliance, with its open spaces, glass facades, and eco-friendly design elements that create a welcoming and sustainable environment.

In addition to its shopping offerings, Lyon Confluence also features cultural attractions such as the Musée des Confluences, a science and anthropology museum that showcases a wide range of exhibits exploring the natural world, human history, and future innovations.
