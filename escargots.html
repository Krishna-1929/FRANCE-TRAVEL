Escargots de Bourgogne (Snails)

Escargots de Bourgogne, or Burgundy snails, are a quintessential French delicacy that showcases the culinary artistry of France. Traditionally prepared with garlic, parsley, and butter, these snails are baked to perfection and served as an elegant appetizer in French cuisine.

The snails are cleaned and cooked in their shells, which are filled with a flavorful garlic and herb butter mixture. The result is a tender and aromatic dish, beloved by locals and adventurous food enthusiasts alike.

Typically served with a special snail fork and tongs, enjoying escargots is as much about the experience as it is about the taste. The rich butter and herb sauce perfectly complements the delicate flavor of the snails.

For an authentic experience, escargots are often paired with a crisp white wine and crusty baguette to soak up the remaining sauce, making every bite memorable.

From fine dining establishments to traditional bistros, Escargots de Bourgogne is a must-try dish for anyone seeking to delve into the unique flavors of French gastronomy.
