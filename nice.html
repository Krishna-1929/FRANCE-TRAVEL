Discover Nice and The French Riviera

Nice

Nice, the capital of the French Riviera, is renowned for its stunning beaches, vibrant culture, and elegant promenade. With its Mediterranean charm, sunny weather, and artistic history, Nice is a jewel of southern France.

The Promenade des Anglais is a famous seaside boulevard in Nice, offering stunning views of the azure waters of the Mediterranean. Perfect for a stroll or a bike ride, it embodies the essence of Riviera glamour.

The Old Town of Nice, or "Vieux Nice," is a labyrinth of narrow streets filled with colorful buildings, bustling markets, and local eateries. It's the heart of the city's history and charm.

The Port of Nice is a picturesque harbor filled with colorful fishing boats and luxury yachts. Surrounded by charming cafes and restaurants, it’s a lovely place to soak in the Riviera vibe.

The French Riviera

The French Riviera, or Côte d'Azur, is a glamorous coastal region known for its luxurious resorts, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant nightlife. Famous destinations include Monaco, Cannes, and Saint-Tropez.

Monte Carlo, in Monaco, is the epitome of luxury with its grand casinos, high-end boutiques, and scenic marina. It's a playground for the rich and famous.

Cannes is renowned for its international film festival, golden beaches, and the famous La Croisette promenade. It’s the perfect blend of culture and luxury.

Saint-Tropez, a former fishing village, is now a symbol of glamour and relaxation. Its charming harbor and sun-soaked beaches make it a must-visit on the Riviera.
