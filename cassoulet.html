Cassoulet

Cassoulet is a traditional French comfort dish that hails from the southwestern regions of France. This hearty and rich stew features white beans slow-cooked with meats like duck, pork, and sausages, creating a savory, satisfying meal perfect for colder days.

Known for its deep flavors, cassoulet is a labor of love, requiring hours of cooking to allow the ingredients to meld together. The result is a dish that embodies the rustic and authentic taste of French country cuisine.

Key ingredients include white beans, duck confit, pork belly, and Toulouse sausages. The preparation varies slightly between regions, with each area adding its unique touch to the dish.
