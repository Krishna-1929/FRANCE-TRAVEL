Experience at Palace of Versailles

The Palace of Versailles, a UNESCO World Heritage site, is a symbol of the grandeur and opulence of the French monarchy. A visit here takes you on a journey through history, art, and culture, offering a glimpse into the lavish lifestyle of the French royals.

Explore the stunning Hall of Mirrors, a breathtaking gallery with 357 mirrors reflecting light and beauty. It’s a masterpiece of architecture and design that represents the splendor of the French monarchy.

The Gardens of Versailles are a masterpiece of landscape architecture. Wander through meticulously designed flowerbeds, fountains, and sculptures that stretch over 800 hectares.

Visit the Grand Trianon, a smaller palace within the estate. This pink marble structure was built as a retreat for Louis XIV and is a stunning example of Baroque architecture.

The estate also includes the Queen’s Hamlet, a picturesque village built for Marie Antoinette, where she could escape the formality of court life and enjoy a simpler, pastoral experience.
