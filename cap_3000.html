Cap 3000

Cap 3000 is one of the largest shopping centers in the French Riviera, located in Saint-Laurent-du-Var, near Nice. With more than 150 shops and boutiques, this modern mall offers a variety of international and French brands, along with dining options, entertainment, and scenic views of the Mediterranean coastline. Cap 3000 is a must-visit destination for both tourists and locals, offering everything from fashion to electronics, and all in a spacious, modern setting.

The shopping center is set along the coastline, offering a beautiful view of the sea and a relaxing shopping experience. Whether you're looking for high-end fashion, home goods, or unique gifts, Cap 3000 has something for everyone.

Inside, you'll find a range of stores from popular fashion retailers to specialty boutiques. Cap 3000 also offers a variety of restaurants and cafes where you can take a break and enjoy the Mediterranean atmosphere.

Cap 3000 is also known for its wide selection of entertainment options, including movie theaters and regular events that draw crowds. It's the perfect place to shop, dine, and enjoy the coastal lifestyle of the French Riviera.
