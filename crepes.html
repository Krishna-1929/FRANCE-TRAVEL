Crêpes

Crêpes are a quintessential French treat, known for their thin and delicate texture. These versatile pancakes can be enjoyed sweet or savory, making them a beloved dish for any time of day.

Sweet crêpes are often filled with ingredients like Nutella, fresh fruits, or whipped cream. They are a delightful dessert or breakfast choice that pairs perfectly with a cup of coffee or tea.

Savory crêpes, known as galettes in Brittany, are typically made with buckwheat flour and filled with ingredients like ham, cheese, and eggs. They make for a hearty and satisfying meal.

Crêpes are a staple of French street food culture, with stands across cities like Paris offering freshly made crêpes to locals and tourists alike. Watching them being prepared is an experience in itself!
