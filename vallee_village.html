La Vallée Village

La Vallée Village is a luxury outlet shopping center located in Marne-la-Vallée, just outside of Paris. Known for offering high-end brands at discounted prices, this charming open-air shopping village is a must-visit for those looking to experience Parisian fashion at a fraction of the price. With over 110 boutiques, La Vallée Village provides a mix of premium brands, exclusive collections, and a relaxed shopping environment.

La Vallée Village features stylish boutiques from top designers and international brands, including fashion, beauty, and accessories. It’s the perfect place for savvy shoppers looking for a blend of luxury and value.

The village is designed like a picturesque town, offering a relaxed and enjoyable shopping experience. The pedestrian-only streets, fountains, and cafés create a welcoming atmosphere for visitors to shop and relax.

La Vallée Village also hosts a variety of events and promotions throughout the year, making it an exciting destination for tourists and locals alike.

The outlet’s proximity to Disneyland Paris also makes it a great stop for tourists looking to pick up some designer goods while enjoying their trip to the theme parks.
