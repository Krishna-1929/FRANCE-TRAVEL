Experience at Dune of Pilat

The Dune of Pilat, located in the Arcachon Bay area, is the tallest sand dune in Europe and a breathtaking natural wonder. Stretching over 100 meters in height and 3 kilometers in length, this incredible site offers stunning views of the Atlantic Ocean, lush pine forests, and the surrounding bay area.

Climbing the Dune of Pilat is an unforgettable experience. The ascent may be challenging, but the reward is worth it—a panoramic view of the endless horizon, golden sands, and emerald-green forest.

Sunset at the Dune of Pilat is a magical experience. The changing hues of the sky and the golden glow of the sand create a serene and picturesque atmosphere, perfect for relaxation or photography.

For thrill-seekers, paragliding off the Dune of Pilat offers a unique perspective of this natural wonder. Soaring through the sky, you’ll enjoy a bird’s-eye view of the spectacular landscape below.

The area around the dune features scenic walking trails and opportunities to explore the nearby forests and beaches. It’s an ideal destination for nature lovers and adventurers alike.
