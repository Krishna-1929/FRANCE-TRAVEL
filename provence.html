Discover Provence

Provence, located in southeastern France, is a region known for its picturesque landscapes, charming villages, and fields of vibrant lavender. A place of timeless beauty, it offers a perfect mix of natural splendor, historical treasures, and culinary delights.

The lavender fields of Provence are world-renowned, blooming from late June to early August. These aromatic fields provide a stunning visual spectacle and are a haven for photographers and nature lovers alike.

The hilltop village of Gordes is one of the most beautiful in Provence. Its cobblestone streets, stone houses, and breathtaking views make it a must-visit destination.

Roussillon, famous for its ochre cliffs, offers a unique palette of warm colors. Stroll through its quaint streets and admire the vibrant, earthy tones that make this village unforgettable.

Sénanque Abbey is a serene Cistercian monastery surrounded by lavender fields. It’s a place of tranquility and beauty that reflects Provence's rich history and spiritual heritage.
