Experience at Château de Chambord

The Château de Chambord is a masterpiece of the French Renaissance and one of the most recognizable castles in the world. Located in the heart of the Loire Valley, this UNESCO World Heritage site offers a glimpse into the grandeur of French history and architecture.

With its stunning architecture, the Château features over 400 rooms, 77 staircases, and an iconic double-helix staircase inspired by Leonardo da Vinci. The intricate design and towering spires make it a marvel to behold.

The estate boasts expansive gardens and a surrounding forest that stretches across 13,000 acres. Visitors can enjoy serene walks, bike rides, or even boat tours in this vast natural area.

Explore the castle’s richly decorated interiors, featuring period furniture, ornate ceilings, and captivating exhibits. The rooftop terrace offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.

Don’t miss the enchanting sight of the Château illuminated at sunset. The golden hues bring out the intricate details of the castle’s façade, creating a magical atmosphere.
