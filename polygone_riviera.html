Polygone Riviera

Polygone Riviera, located in Cagnes-sur-Mer on the French Riviera, is a large outdoor shopping mall offering a mix of fashion, dining, and entertainment. With its open-air layout, it allows visitors to enjoy the beautiful Mediterranean weather while shopping at high-end and luxury boutiques. This mall is one of the most popular shopping destinations on the French Riviera, perfect for those looking to indulge in style and comfort.

Featuring more than 100 stores, Polygone Riviera combines modern architecture with a welcoming atmosphere, making it a vibrant destination for both locals and tourists. Whether you're seeking high-end brands or more affordable options, the shopping center offers a wide range of choices.

Beyond shopping, the center also offers a variety of dining options from gourmet restaurants to casual cafes. Visitors can take a break from shopping and enjoy delicious Mediterranean cuisine or a refreshing drink in one of the many outdoor seating areas.

The mall also offers regular cultural events and exhibitions, creating a lively and dynamic atmosphere. It's the perfect place to spend the day, whether you're shopping, dining, or simply enjoying the Mediterranean vibes.
