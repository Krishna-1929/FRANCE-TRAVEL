Les Quatre Temps

Les Quatre Temps is one of the largest shopping centers in the Paris region, located in La Défense, just outside the city center. Opened in 1981, this modern mall boasts over 230 shops, ranging from high-end fashion brands to everyday convenience stores. With its sleek architecture and wide range of services, it’s the perfect destination for shopping, dining, and entertainment in the business district of La Défense.

Les Quatre Temps is well known for its contemporary design, and the shopping center is often visited by locals, tourists, and business professionals working in the nearby office buildings. The shopping mall is easily accessible via the La Défense metro station and the RER A line.

With a vast selection of stores, Les Quatre Temps caters to every shopper's needs. Whether you're looking for the latest fashion trends, tech gadgets, or home décor, this mall offers a variety of choices. It’s also home to numerous cafes and restaurants, perfect for taking a break after a shopping spree.

In addition to shopping, Les Quatre Temps offers a range of entertainment options, including a cinema and cultural events throughout the year. The mall provides a unique blend of work, leisure, and retail, making it a must-visit for anyone in the La Défense area.
