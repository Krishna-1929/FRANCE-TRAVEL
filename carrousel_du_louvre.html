Carrousel du Louvre

Located beneath the Louvre Museum in Paris, the Carrousel du Louvre is a shopping mall that blends art, culture, and commerce. The center is housed in a beautiful space, with stunning architecture and a modern design. With its combination of luxury boutiques, galleries, and dining options, it’s the perfect destination for both shopping and cultural exploration.

The Carrousel du Louvre is connected to the famous glass pyramid at the entrance of the Louvre Museum. It offers a variety of luxury shops, galleries, and even a few unique boutiques that reflect the elegant Parisian style. It’s an ideal spot for visitors looking to experience the charm of Parisian retail while staying close to the Louvre's world-class art collection.

In addition to shopping, the Carrousel du Louvre features stunning architecture with modern touches, making it a memorable stop for those interested in Parisian design. The space is open and airy, with natural light pouring in from the glass pyramid above, adding to the exceptional ambiance of this unique shopping destination.

If you're interested in art, the Carrousel also features temporary art exhibitions that add an extra cultural layer to your visit. The variety of exhibits is always changing, showcasing everything from contemporary art to historical collections.
