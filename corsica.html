Discover Corsica

Corsica is a stunning Mediterranean island known for its rugged landscape, crystal-clear waters, and unique blend of French and Italian culture. From dramatic mountain peaks to charming coastal towns, Corsica offers something for every kind of traveler. Its natural beauty and rich history make it a perfect destination for adventure seekers and relaxation lovers alike.

Cap Corse is the northernmost point of Corsica, featuring dramatic cliffs and stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea. A perfect destination for hiking and photography, Cap Corse offers a glimpse into the island's rugged beauty.

Bonifacio, perched on the southern tip of the island, is a historic town known for its stunning cliffs and ancient citadel. The old town offers winding streets, a charming harbor, and breathtaking views of the sea.

Sartène is a picturesque town in southern Corsica, often considered one of the most authentic and traditional places on the island. With its narrow streets, stone houses, and rich Corsican culture, Sartène provides a fascinating glimpse into the island's past.

Plage de Bonifacio is a pristine beach located near Bonifacio, known for its turquoise waters and fine white sand. It’s the perfect spot to relax or indulge in water sports like snorkeling and diving.

The mountainous terrain of Corsica offers a haven for hikers and outdoor enthusiasts. With over 1200 kilometers of hiking trails, the island is a paradise for those looking to explore its natural wonders, including the famous GR20 trail.
