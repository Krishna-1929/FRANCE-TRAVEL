Discover Versailles

The Palace of Versailles is a breathtaking symbol of opulence and grandeur, reflecting the peak of French royal luxury. Located just outside Paris, it has stood as a UNESCO World Heritage site since 1979 and remains a testament to the splendor of the French monarchy.

The Hall of Mirrors is a must-see attraction with its dazzling reflections, stunning chandeliers, and golden embellishments. It was the site of the signing of the Treaty of Versailles, marking the end of World War I.

The meticulously designed gardens by André Le Nôtre are a visual feast, with fountains, sculptures, and geometric flowerbeds creating a harmonious blend of nature and artistry.

Explore the Trianon Estate, including the Grand Trianon, Petit Trianon, and Marie Antoinette’s Hamlet, offering a serene escape from the grandeur of the main palace.
